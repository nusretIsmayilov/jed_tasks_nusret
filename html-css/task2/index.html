Music
Music is an art form whose medium is sound and silence, Generally, a song is considered the smallest standalone work of music, especially when involving singing.
The common elements of music are pitch (which governs melody and harmony), rhythm (and its associated concepts tempo, meter, and articulation), dynamics, and the sonic qualities of timbre and texture. The word derives from Greek μouaikη (mousike, "art of the Muses").
